Welcome

    Last Stop Computers is your last hope for getting your electionics back into action! We are a electronics store in the Tacoma area. Our repair shop provides a wide range of repairs both hardware and software.

    Have no idea what's wrong? No problem! Just stop by, give us a call or send us an email and we'll figure it out together.
About us

    Starting out as a hobby in the late 80's by its Owner Brian Herbert. Last Stop Computers opened its first Shop in 1992 on 6th ave. Focusing on quality computer builds and support, the current location was purchased in 2001 and has been growing ever since. We have never advertised and rely on word of mouth to keep our business. We offer phone support and maintance both in the bussiness and home user markets.
